Sausage Gravy

Description

This is, by far, my most favorite sausage gravy recipe. I have since shared this recipe with my mother and even she says that she'll never make biscuits and gravy the old way again. It's so easy and packed with flavor. I think what I love most is the little kick you get from the hot sausage and added cayenne pepper. If you can't find Jimmy Dean Hot Sausage, you can use regular sausage and add some red chili flakes and it will taste just the same.

Ingredients

  • 12oz. Jimmy Dean Hot Sausage
  • 3 1/2 C 2% or whole milk
  • 3 T Butter
  • 1/3 C Flour
  • 1/2 t Salt
  • 1/2 t Pepper
  • 1/4 t cayenne

Directions

  1. Heat butter in a large frying pan over medium-hi until foaming
  2. Add sausage, cook until brown
  3. Reduce to medium heat, sprinkle with flour, salt, pepper, and cayenne
  4. Stir about 1 minute to cook off flour taste
  5. Gradually stir in milk, scraping the pan
  6. Bring to a simmer and stir until thick
  7. Serve over your favorite biscuits
